About Us

A Family Business
Built on Trust.

Established in 1976, Oriental Printers is a proudly family-owned printing business rooted in the heart of White City, Port Shepstone. With nearly five decades of experience, we have grown into one of the most trusted names in printing and branding across KwaZulu-Natal, offering a blend of traditional values and modern solutions.

At Oriental Printers, we believe that printing is more than ink on paper — it's about making your vision come to life. Whether it's a banner that commands attention, a business card that leaves a lasting impression, or a booklet that tells your story with clarity and style, we bring our passion, expertise, and dedication to every project. Our motto, "Your Concept, Our Creation," is more than just a slogan — it's the philosophy that drives everything we do.
