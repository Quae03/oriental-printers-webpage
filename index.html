About

Established in 1976, Oriental Printers is a proudly family-owned printing business rooted in the heart of White City, Port Shepstone. With nearly five decades of experience, we have grown into one of the most trusted names in printing and branding across KwaZulu-Natal, offering a blend of traditional values and modern solutions.

At Oriental Printers, we believe that printing is more than ink on paper—it's about making your vision come to life. Whether it's a banner that commands attention, a business card that leaves a lasting impression, or a booklet that tells your story with clarity and style, we bring our passion, expertise, and dedication to every project. Our motto, "Your Concept, Our Creation," is more than just a slogan—it's the philosophy that drives everything we do.

We specialize in a wide range of services to meet your every printing and design need. Our offerings include graphic design, branding, banners, business cards, booklets, calendars, certificates, flyers, funeral printing, general typing, invites, laminating, letterheads, pads, NCR books, posters, rubber stamps, signage, stickers, tickets, vinyl, correx boards, and even T-shirt printing (on white 100% polyester). Whether you're a small business, a large corporation, or an individual with a personal project, we're here to make it happen.

Over the years, we've built a reputation for quality, affordability, and a personal touch. As a family-run business, we value relationships. Many of our clients have been with us for years, trusting us with everything from everyday print needs to urgent funeral programs and high-impact promotional materials. We treat every job—big or small—with the same level of care and professionalism.

We offer both digital and lithographic printing, ensuring flexibility and precision across all formats and quantities. From high-volume orders to single-piece custom designs, we have the equipment, skill, and creativity to deliver on time and beyond expectations.

Conveniently located at 8 Sunflower Road, White City, Port Shepstone, our doors are open to walk-ins, phone orders, and email inquiries. We strive to offer a seamless customer experience—from consultation and design to printing and finishing. Our friendly team is always ready to assist, advise, and ensure you get exactly what you envision.

As we continue to evolve, we stay committed to innovation while staying true to our roots. We understand the importance of staying up-to-date with the latest trends in design and printing technology, but never at the expense of the personal service that has defined us since day one.
